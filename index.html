Field Survey

This is a general survey of the field to find out its basic information by using our patented kit.
An agent is sent to check your entire field with this kit to identify the concentrations of minerals and other nutrients in the soil.
The agent then gives information about how to increase the yield of the field by prescribing medicines and our Organic Manure.

Smart Irrigation Systems

Irrigation systems of sprinklers are installed as per the requirement to make sure that the field remains well- watered.
Sensors will be spread across the field to detect the lack of moisture in different places.
These sensors will then automatically activate the sprinklers in the area where irrigation is required.
Therefore, it reduces wastage of water and manual labour

Disease Detection

If the farmer observes some disease in the crops or low yield, the agent can be called who will be able to identify the disease and suggest the medication required to overcome it quickly.
This will enable the farmer to quickly provide the necessary treatment for the crops to avoid loss of yield.

Fire Detection Kit

To prevent any loss from accidental fires, multiple fire sensors will be installed at various locations spread across the field.
These sensors will then trigger an alarm, alerting the farmer by call and message, while also activating any sprinklers in the area, if installed.
This will help in avoiding any loss of crop due to fire damage.

Complete Sensor Protection Kit

Following services are provided in this plan:

  • 4 field surveys
  • 2 disease detections
  • Smart irrigation system
  • Fire detection system
